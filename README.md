📌 Project Overview
ShopLedger is a voice-powered AI bookkeeping application designed specifically for micro-traders and MSMEs within Nigeria's informal market spaces. Developed by Team Unique-UNIMAID (University of Maiduguri) for the OPay National Innovation Challenge 2026, this platform allows informal merchants to seamlessly track transactions using native speech pattern logic (Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, or English) to remove barriers to financial inclusion.

🛠️ Step-by-Step Examiner Evaluation Guide

Follow these sequential steps on the live link to experience the full features of our application:

1. Test Voice & Speech Logic (`Record` Tab)
Select a Language: Use the horizontal language scroll bar at the top to select your preferred language (e.g., English or Hausa).
Test Real Microphone (Google Chrome Recommended on laptop): Click the large teal mic button. Speak a trade phrase naturally (e.g., "I sold 3 bags of rice for 15,000 naira"). Tap the button again to stop recording, view the text extraction, and click ✅ Save Transaction.
Test Clickable Examples: If microphone permissions are unavailable, scroll down and tap any of our preset market phrases under "Or tap an example below".
Observe Smart AI Extraction: Notice how our algorithm instantly categorizes the phrase into standard accounting sheets, assigns high/medium confidence metrics, and formats currency records automatically.

2. Verify Double-Entry Records (`Ledger` Tab)
Tap the Ledger tab on the bottom navigation bar.
  Verify how your recorded speech inputs are instantly logged into an organized income/expense spreadsheet with active color-coded visual cues (Green for Income, Red for Expenses) alongside data timestamp markers.

3. Review Infographics & Credit Scores (`Report` Tab)
  Head back to the Record tab and click 📊 Generate Weekly Report.
  You will be taken directly to the Report dashboard. Check the dynamic KPI cards displaying Income, Expenses, and Net Profits.
  Scroll to view the AI Business Advice Engine which pairs English feedback with localized native translation cards.
  Review the OPay Credit Score Simulator. It uses transaction activity to map out structural loan eligibility and unlocks mock credit pathways up to ₦500,000 based on recorded proof.

4. Interactive Pitching Tools (`Script` & `Present` Tabs)
🎬 Script Tab: Explore our fully functional video presentation teleprompter built to assist team media recording workflows. Toggle through presentation segments (Hook, Solution, Impact, Team, Close) and adjust playback speeds (Slow, Normal, Fast).
  🖥️ Present Tab: Click 🚀 Launch Presentation to initiate the high-fidelity projector slide deck template configured specifically for challenge judges. Use your keyboard arrow keys (`←` / `→`) or browser tap points to review our structured product slides natively.
